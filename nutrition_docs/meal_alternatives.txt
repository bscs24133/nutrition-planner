MEAL INGREDIENT ALTERNATIVES - SUBSTITUTION GUIDE

PROTEIN ALTERNATIVES:
- No chicken? Use: turkey, eggs, lentils, chickpeas, tofu, paneer, tuna
- No eggs? Use: tofu scramble, Greek yogurt, chia seeds, flax seeds
- No fish? Use: chicken, tofu, legumes, nuts
- No beef? Use: lamb, chicken, lentils, mushrooms (for texture)
- No paneer? Use: tofu, halloumi, cottage cheese, ricotta
- No Greek yogurt? Use: regular yogurt strained, cottage cheese, silken tofu

CARBOHYDRATE ALTERNATIVES:
- No quinoa? Use: brown rice, bulgur wheat, couscous, barley, oats
- No brown rice? Use: quinoa, cauliflower rice, whole wheat pasta, barley
- No oats? Use: muesli, whole wheat bread, bran flakes, overnight barley
- No sweet potato? Use: regular potato, butternut squash, carrots, parsnip
- No whole wheat bread? Use: rye bread, sourdough, oat bread, rice cakes
- No pasta? Use: zucchini noodles, rice noodles, soba noodles, cauliflower

VEGETABLE ALTERNATIVES:
- No spinach? Use: kale, Swiss chard, arugula, pak choi, collard greens
- No broccoli? Use: cauliflower, Brussels sprouts, green beans, asparagus
- No avocado? Use: hummus, olive oil, nut butter (for healthy fats)
- No bell peppers? Use: zucchini, eggplant, tomatoes, mushrooms
- No lettuce? Use: spinach, arugula, cabbage, kale, endive

FAT ALTERNATIVES:
- No olive oil? Use: avocado oil, coconut oil (in moderation), canola oil
- No avocado? Use: hummus, tahini, nut butter for healthy fats
- No nuts? Use: seeds (sunflower, pumpkin, sesame), nut butters

DAIRY ALTERNATIVES (for lactose intolerant):
- No milk? Use: almond milk, oat milk, soy milk, coconut milk
- No butter? Use: olive oil, coconut oil, avocado, nut butter
- No cheese? Use: nutritional yeast (cheesy flavor), dairy-free cheese
- No yogurt? Use: coconut yogurt, soy yogurt, cashew yogurt

HALAL ALTERNATIVES:
- No pork? Use: chicken, beef, lamb, turkey (all halal certified)
- Avoid: gelatin (check labels), vanilla extract with alcohol, some E-numbers
- Safe proteins: all fish, seafood, eggs, dairy, all plants, halal-certified meats

GLUTEN-FREE ALTERNATIVES:
- No wheat flour? Use: rice flour, almond flour, coconut flour, oat flour
- No regular pasta? Use: rice pasta, corn pasta, chickpea pasta, lentil pasta
- No bread? Use: rice cakes, gluten-free bread, corn tortillas, lettuce wraps
- No oats? Use: certified gluten-free oats, buckwheat, millet, quinoa
