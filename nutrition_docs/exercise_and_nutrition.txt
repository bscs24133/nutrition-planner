EXERCISE AND NUTRITION GUIDE

PRE-WORKOUT NUTRITION:
- Eat 1-3 hours before exercise for energy
- Focus on carbohydrates for fuel, some protein
- Pre-workout meal examples:
  * Oats with banana and peanut butter
  * Brown rice with chicken
  * Whole wheat toast with eggs
  * Greek yogurt with berries and granola
- Avoid: high fat meals, very high fiber, large portions right before

POST-WORKOUT NUTRITION:
- Eat within 30-60 minutes after exercise
- Focus on protein (muscle repair) and carbs (glycogen refill)
- Post-workout meal examples:
  * Protein shake with banana
  * Chicken with rice and vegetables
  * Eggs with toast and vegetables
  * Tuna with whole grain crackers
  * Greek yogurt with fruit

HYDRATION DURING EXERCISE:
- Drink 500ml water 2 hours before exercise
- Drink 150-250ml every 15-20 minutes during exercise
- Drink 500ml for every 0.5kg lost during exercise after
- For exercise over 60 minutes add electrolytes (sodium, potassium)
- Signs of dehydration during exercise: dizziness, cramps, dark urine

NUTRITION FOR DIFFERENT EXERCISE TYPES:

STRENGTH TRAINING / WEIGHT LIFTING:
- Higher protein intake: 1.6-2.2g per kg body weight
- Enough carbs to fuel workouts and recovery
- Creatine may help performance (5g daily)
- Progressive overload + adequate protein = muscle growth

CARDIO / ENDURANCE TRAINING:
- Higher carbohydrate needs for fuel
- Moderate protein for muscle maintenance
- Important to replenish glycogen after long sessions
- For sessions over 90 minutes use sports drinks or gels

YOGA AND FLEXIBILITY:
- Eat light before sessions to avoid discomfort
- Focus on overall balanced nutrition
- Anti-inflammatory foods help recovery: turmeric, ginger, berries

SUPPLEMENTS (COMMONLY ASKED):
- Protein powder: useful if struggling to meet protein from food
  Best types: whey (fast absorbing), casein (slow, good before bed),
  pea protein (vegan option)
- Creatine: safe and effective for strength, 5g daily
- Vitamin D: many people deficient, especially in low-sun climates
- Omega-3: anti-inflammatory, supports heart and brain health
- Caffeine: improves performance, 3-6mg per kg body weight
- Avoid: unregulated fat burners, extreme pre-workouts, anything
  promising rapid results (usually dangerous or fake)

TYPES OF EXERCISE AND THEIR BENEFITS:
- Walking (30 min/day): reduces heart disease risk by 30%
- Strength training (2-3x/week): increases metabolism, bone density
- Swimming: full body, low impact, great for joints
- Cycling: cardiovascular health, leg strength, low impact
- HIIT: burns fat efficiently in short time, boosts metabolism
- Yoga: flexibility, stress reduction, mental health
- Running: strong cardiovascular system, mood boost, weight control
