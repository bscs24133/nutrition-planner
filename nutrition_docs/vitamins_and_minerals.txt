VITAMINS AND MINERALS COMPLETE GUIDE

VITAMIN A:
- Function: vision, immune system, skin health
- Sources: liver, eggs, dairy, orange vegetables (carrots, sweet potato)
- Deficiency: night blindness, dry skin, poor immunity

VITAMIN B12:
- Function: nerve function, red blood cells, DNA
- Sources: meat, fish, eggs, dairy ONLY (not found in plants)
- Deficiency risk: vegans, vegetarians, elderly
- Symptoms of deficiency: fatigue, numbness, memory problems
- Vegans MUST supplement vitamin B12

VITAMIN C:
- Function: immune system, collagen, iron absorption
- Sources: citrus fruits, strawberries, bell peppers, broccoli
- Boosts immune system and helps iron absorption from plants
- Cannot be stored - eat daily

VITAMIN D:
- Function: bone health, immune system, mood regulation
- Sources: sunlight, fatty fish, egg yolks, fortified foods
- Most common deficiency worldwide especially in South Asia
- Supplement 1000-2000 IU daily if limited sun exposure

VITAMIN E:
- Function: antioxidant, protects cells, skin health
- Sources: nuts, seeds, vegetable oils, leafy greens

VITAMIN K:
- Function: blood clotting, bone health
- Sources: leafy greens, broccoli, Brussels sprouts, fermented foods

IRON:
- Function: carries oxygen in blood
- Sources: red meat, lentils, spinach, fortified cereals
- Two types: heme iron (meat - better absorbed),
  non-heme iron (plants - less absorbed)
- Eat with vitamin C to increase plant iron absorption
- Avoid tea/coffee with iron-rich meals (reduces absorption)
- Vegetarians at higher risk - need to focus on plant iron sources

CALCIUM:
- Function: bones, teeth, muscle contraction, nerve signals
- Sources: dairy, fortified plant milk, sardines, tofu, leafy greens
- Adults need 1000mg daily
- Vitamin D needed for calcium absorption

MAGNESIUM:
- Function: 300+ enzyme reactions, sleep, muscle function
- Sources: nuts, seeds, whole grains, leafy greens, dark chocolate
- Deficiency very common: causes poor sleep, muscle cramps, anxiety

ZINC:
- Function: immune system, wound healing, taste and smell
- Sources: meat, shellfish, legumes, nuts, seeds, dairy
- Vegetarians at risk of deficiency

POTASSIUM:
- Function: blood pressure control, muscle contraction, heart function
- Sources: bananas, sweet potato, avocado, spinach, beans
- Helps counteract high sodium effects on blood pressure

OMEGA-3 FATTY ACIDS:
- Function: brain health, inflammation reduction, heart health
- Sources: fatty fish (salmon, sardines, mackerel), walnuts, flaxseeds
- Most people do not get enough omega-3
- Consider supplement if do not eat fatty fish 2-3x per week
