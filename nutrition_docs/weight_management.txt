WEIGHT LOSS AND WEIGHT GAIN GUIDE

WEIGHT LOSS:
- Create a calorie deficit of 500 calories per day for 0.5kg loss per week
- Never go below 1200 calories (women) or 1500 calories (men)
- Eat more protein to preserve muscle while losing fat
- Prioritize whole foods over processed foods
- Eat slowly - takes 20 minutes for brain to register fullness
- Do not skip breakfast - increases hunger and cravings later
- Drink water before meals to naturally reduce portion size
- Sleep 7-9 hours - poor sleep increases hunger hormones
- Limit liquid calories: juice, soda, alcohol, sugary coffee
- Best weight loss foods: eggs, lean chicken, vegetables,
  legumes, Greek yogurt, oats, berries, green tea

MUSCLE GAIN:
- Eat calorie surplus of 200-300 calories above maintenance
- Consume 1.6-2.2g protein per kg body weight daily
- Eat carbohydrates before and after workouts for energy and recovery
- Do not fear healthy fats - they support hormone production
- Eat every 3-4 hours to keep muscle protein synthesis elevated
- Best muscle building foods: eggs, chicken, beef, salmon, rice,
  oats, sweet potato, milk, Greek yogurt, nuts, legumes

HEALTHY WEIGHT MAINTENANCE:
- Eat balanced meals with protein, carbs, and fat at each meal
- Follow 80/20 rule: eat healthy 80% of the time
- Monitor portions without strict calorie counting
- Stay physically active with mix of cardio and strength training
- Weigh yourself weekly at same time for accurate tracking

COMMON WEIGHT LOSS MISTAKES:
- Eating too little (slows metabolism, causes muscle loss)
- Avoiding all carbs (causes fatigue, cravings, unsustainable)
- Skipping meals (leads to overeating later)
- Drinking calories (soda, juice, alcohol add up fast)
- Only doing cardio (strength training is essential for metabolism)
- Expecting fast results (healthy rate is 0.5-1kg per week)
